About me

I am a 2nd year Master student who majors in the Control Engineering at the Department of Automation, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My advisor is Prof. Xiang Yin.

I received my B.S. degree in Automation from Beihang University in Jul. 2019. During Jan. 2018-Jul. 2019, I worked with Prof. Fei Tao and Prof. Ying Cheng as an undergraduate research assistant.

My current reseach interests lie in systems and control theory, discrete-event systems and formal methods.
